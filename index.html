Here's What's Going On!

Recently: A new species of creatures has popped up. They wear furs and are mostly covered in just skin, but are highly intelligent. We need to investigate, and if they need help, we'll have to try helping these new animals.

History as of Now

The world of Elra was created, and dragons were the first intelligent beings to be put in it. Diox was among the first dragons and became the first king. They are on their fifth generation. The current king is Tarox the Gentle and his queen is Magmentia. Their son is Raulk.
